ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Glenn Dyer

Glenn Dyer writes the Conor Thorn series—WWII espionage thrillers praised by Steve Berry, Jack Carr, and Chris Wallace. His five novels combine meticulous archival research with pulse-pounding suspense, placing readers inside history's most dangerous secret operations.

Each book in the series is grounded in declassified intelligence records and firsthand wartime accounts—"a perfect fit for fans of Alan Furst."
